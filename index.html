Meet Chef Maryann Achime

The Heart Behind Every Meal.

Chow Express Nigeria, known as @chow_express_lagos_ on Instagram, is a proudly Lagos-based food brand founded in 2019 by Maryann Achime — lovingly known as Chef Maryann Achime. Born from a passion for authentic Nigerian cuisine and a mission to eliminate kitchen stress, Chow Express has grown into one of Lagos's most trusted food vendors.

Operating from the heart of Sangotedo, Ajah, Chef Maryann Achime and her team specialise in freshly made Nigerian meals — cooked from scratch, seasoned to perfection, and delivered straight to your door. From the rich depth of Seafood Okra to the bold flavours of Fisherman Soup, every dish carries the warmth of a home-cooked meal.

With a "freshly made-from-scratch" philosophy, Chow Express serves not just food but an experience — offering food bowls, event catering, and bulk orders for birthdays, weddings, and corporate gatherings across Lagos.
